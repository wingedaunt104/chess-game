chess piece info and controls

The most numerous piece in the game and can only move forward and attack diagonally, also if a queen is eliminated you can use a pawn to get all the way to the end of the board and spawn another queen(opening move can be two squares)

Each player gets two bishops. They move/attack diagonally by any number of squares and are restricted to light or dark squares for the entire match

Each player gets two knights. They move in an L shape left, right, vertically, and horizontally, knights can jump over other pieces

Each player has two rooks, rooks can move only vertically and horizontally by any amount of squares

Each player has one king which they need to DEFEND DEFEND DEFEND. while defending your own king you must also make an effort to checkmate your opponent's king

Each player has one queen, the queen is the most powerful piece on the board by default, the queen can move and attack in any direction by any number of squares

To move pieces just left click on the piece you wish to use then left click where you want that piece to go on the borad or attack
